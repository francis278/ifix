My-T-Soft Panel Motion Dialog Box

Panel Motion

Choose "Snap-Out Panel" and My-T-Soft's panels will snap in & out. The new configuration will be displayed without intermediate displays.

Choose "Slide-Out Panel" and My-T-Soft's panels will slide in & out. The new configuration will be displayed with a visual effect of motion.

Press the OK Button.

If you do not want to save your changes, press the CANCEL button.

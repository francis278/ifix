Chapter 4. My-T-Soft Menu

The My-T-Soft Menu will give you quick access to the most used functions of My-T-Soft and Windows System Menu functions. The default operation of the right mouse button (i.e. second button) will open this menu when it is clicked over the My-T-Soft window.

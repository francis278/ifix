Quick Help Panel

The Quick Help panel appears above the currently displayed panels and describes each key as it is passed over with the cursor. You may open or close the Quick Help panel from the My-T-Soft Menu or from the Help button on the My-T-Soft Control Panel .

To assign the Quick Help capability to your macros, see How to create Macros in My-T-Soft Setup Help.
